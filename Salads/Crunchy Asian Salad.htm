Crunchy Asian Salad

1 envelope Good Seasons Italian Salad Dressing

1/2 cup Sugar

2 Tablespoons Soy Sauce

2 Packages Dry Ramen Noodle Soup Mix

2 (16 ounce) Packages Coleslaw Blend

4 Sliced Green Onions

1/2 cup Planters Dry Roasted Sunflower Kernels

1/2 cup Toasted Planters Sliced Almonds

Prepare Good Seasons dressing un a bowl as directed on envelope.

Stir in Sugar and soy sauce.

Break apart noodles and place in a bowl.

Add coleslaw blend, green onions and nuts.

Add dressing and toss to coat.

Serve immediately.
