Green Goddess Dip

2 Cups Shredded Mozzorella

2 Cups Shredded Cheddar

1 Cup Diced Green Pepper

1 Cup Chopped Green Onion

1 Cup Chopped Tomatoes

1 Small Can Chopped Black Olivies

Green Goddess Dressing

Mix first four ingredents.

Add tomatoes and dressing just prior to serving.

Serve with scoop chips.
