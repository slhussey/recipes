Chocolate Covered Cookie Dough Bites

1 1/4 cups flour

1/2 cup brown sugar

1/2 cup butter, softened

1/4 cup sugar

2 Tablespoons powdered sugar

2 teaspoons vanilla

8 ounces mini chocolate chips

8 ounces milk chocolate

8 ounces white chocolate

1/2 cup toffee bits (if desired)

finely chopped nuts (if desired)

Cream brown sugar, white sugar and butter.

Add flour, powdered sugar and vanilla.

Add mini chocolate chips.

Roll into small balls and place on lined cookie sheet.

Chill at least 30 minutes.

Melt white chocolate.

Dip cookie dough balls into white chocolate.

Let harden.

Melt milk chocolate.

Dip cookie dough balls into milk chocolate.

Sprinkle immediately with toffee bits and/or nuts (if desired).

Let harden again.

Any left over chocolate can be drizzled over balls as decoration.
