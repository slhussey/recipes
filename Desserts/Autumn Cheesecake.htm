Autumn Cheesecake

1 cup graham cracker crumbs

3 Tablespoons sugar

1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

1/4 cup butter, melted

2 8-ounce packages cream cheese, softened

1/2 cup sugar

2 eggs

1/2 teaspoon vanilla

4 cups apples, peeled and thinly sliced

1/3 cup sugar

1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

1/4 cup chopped pecans

Combine crumbs, sugar, cinnamon and butter.

Press into 9-inch pan

Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes.

Combine cheese and sugar.

Mix at medium speed until well blended.

Add eggs and vanilla.

Pour over crust.

Top apples with sugar and cinnamon.

Spoon apples over cream cheese layer.

Sprinkle with pecans.

Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour and 10 minutes.

Loosen cake from side of pan: cool before removing.

Chill.
