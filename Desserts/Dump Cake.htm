Dump Cake

20 ounce can crushed pineapple

21 ounce can apple, cherry or blueberry pie filling

1 box yellow cake mix

1 stick margarine or butter

Crushed pecans/walnuts (optional)

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

In a 9 X 13 pan, empty crushed pineapple (do not drain), then add pie filling.

Spread the unprepared yellow cake mix on top of filling.

Either melt margarine and drizzle over cake mix or chop up and drop on the cake mix.

Add pecans/walnuts to the top of the cake if desired

Cook at 350 degrees for 50 - 60 minutes.
