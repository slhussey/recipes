Death by Chocolate

1 Brownie mix

4 1/2 cups chocolate mousse

2 8-ounce cool whip (or dream whip)

8 Skor bars (chopped)

1/2 cup Kahula

Make brownie mix as shown on the box.

Let brownies cool.

Poke holes in brownies and pour Kahula over brownies.

Let stand about 1/2 hour.

In large bowl, layer the ingredents as follows:

1. brownies

2. chocolate mousse

3. cool whip

4. chopped skor bars

Continue layering ingredients until they are all gone.
