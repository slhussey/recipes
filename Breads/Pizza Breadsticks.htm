Pizza Breadsticks

1 package (11 ounces) refrigerated French bread dough

olive oil

2 garlic cloves, pressed

1 1/2 ounces (about 1/3 cup) Parmesan or Romano cheese, grated

1/2 cup shredded mozzzarella cheese

1 teaspoon dried oregano leaves

1 can (8 ounces) pizza sauce

Preheat oven to 375 degrees.

Unroll dough onto baking stone.

Lightly spray olive oil over dough.

Press garlic over dough, spreading evenly.

Sprinkle Paremsan and mozzarella cheeses and oregano over dough.

Bake 12-14 minutes or until golden brown.

Heat pizza sauce in microwave on HIGH for 1-1 1/2 minutes or until heated through.

Cut bread into long strips.

Serve pizza sauce for dipping.

Yield: 20 breadsticks
