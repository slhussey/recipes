Mocha Punch

1 quart chocolate milk

4 cups strong brewed coffee, chilled

1 cup Kahlua or other coffee-flavored liqueur*

1 (14-ounce) can sweetened condensed milk

1 quart chocolate ice cream

1 quart coffee ice cream

Semisweet chocolate shavings

Combine first 4 ingredients to a large pitcher.

Cover and chill.

Pour chilled mixture into a large punch bowl.

scoop chocolate and coffee ice creams into punch; stir gently.

Sprinkle with chocolate shavings.

Yield: 4 1/2 quarts.

* Substitute amaretto-flavored non-dairy liquid creamer for Kahlua, or increase coffee to 5 cups.
