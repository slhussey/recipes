Margarita Punch

1 bottle margarita coctail mix (with tequila)

2 16-ounce bottles of club soda

1 qt lemon or lime sherbert

4 cups orange juice

Maraschino cherries

Pour OJ into ring mold; add cherries (drained) and freeze.

Pour mix into punch bowl.

Add sherbert and mix until it just breaks apart.

Add OJ ring.

Just before serving add club soda.
