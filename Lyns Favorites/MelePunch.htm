Mele Kalikimaka Punch

Submitted by: Mischele Hicks

Source: Hawaiian Recipes, accessed 6/14/2004

1 can frozen cranberry juice concentrate

1 can frozen lemonade concentrate

2 liters lemon-lime soda

1 bottle sparkling mineral water

1 package frozen raspberries

Combine all ingredients in a large pitcher or punch bowl and stir. Pour in a glass over ice and enjoy.
