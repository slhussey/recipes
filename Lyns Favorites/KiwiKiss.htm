Kiwi Kiss

Yield: approx. 4 cups

Source: Smoothies - 50 Recipes for High-Energy Refreshment, 1999 by Mary Corpening Barber, Sara Corpening and Lori Lyn Narlock

1¾ cups diced fresh kiwi

1½ cups lime sherbet

1 cup diced fresh honeydew melon

Place all ingredients in a blender. Process until smooth.
